Appointment Management System

This module allows you to schedule an appointment and manage for your customers and clients in Odoo.

The Odoo user can create appointee and arrange them in various groups. Each appointee can be given appropriate appointment slots to prevent double bookings on the same slot.

Once an appointment is approved, the invoice can be generated in the Odoo. The record of every scheduled appointment is maintained in the Odoo.

The Odoo user can also enable reminder mail to be sent to the customer to notify him/her days/hours before the date of the scheduled appointment

Highlighted Features

  • Appointment Management System can be used to arrange meetings and appointments.
  • User can book an appointment for a customer with an appointee.
  • On booking an appointment user can provide an appointment number to the customer.
  • User can provide a Date and Timeslot to the customer after booking an appointment.
  • Facility to download and print a Report of a Booked Appointment.
  • Groups can be created for a similar type of Appointees.
  • Appointees can be created and added to their Groups.
  • The module allows the feature to create Timeslots and assign to the Appointees.
  • Provides Mail Notification facility to send a Reminder before an Appointment.
  • Admin can pre-define some products in order to add charges to an appointment.
  • User can add the lines in Appointment to apply Charges and taxes in the booked appointment.
  • Appointment Status is maintained on each action taken.
  • User can Approve or Reject the Appointment.

Now, Customers can book their own Appointments

Appointments reduce hassle, overcrowded business place and save time and resources for both you and your customers. Do not just book appointments for your customers, instead also allow your website customers to book their appointments directly from the Odoo website using Odoo Website Appointment Management System module.

Key Pointers

  • The customers can book the appointments from the Odoo website.
  • The appointees can be seen from the website while booking the appointments.
  • Reduces the time taken to call up and inquire about the availability of the appointee and time slot. All is visible on the website.
  • Saves resources and time for both the parties i.e you and your customers.

Now, send SMS notifications for Appointments to Customers

Appointment SMS Notification Management Module is specifically developed to keep your customers up to date about the progress of their appointment requests. Once configured, it sends automatic SMS notifications to your customers. It also sends reminders to notify about an upcoming appointment. Get the module and reach out to your customers on their mobile phones.

Key Pointers

  • Send SMS notifications about successful appointment request generated and change in status of appointment request.
  • Send reminders about an upcoming appointment. Select the days/hours before the date of appointment to send the reminders.
  • View the record of every notification and reminder sent to each customers in the Odoo.
