Asterisk Calls CRM addon

Integrate your CRM app with Calls!

Watch the video

Features:

  • Adds calls statistics and recordings to leads and opportunities.
  • Click to call from leads and opportunities.
  • Assign leads to calls, lead field in active channels / calls history form.
  • Create opportunities from calls with a click.
  • Auto create leads for incoming calls for unknown callers.
  • Auto open lead's form on incoming call.

Please note!
This module requires Asterisk Calls application.
Visit Asterisk Calls application for more information.
